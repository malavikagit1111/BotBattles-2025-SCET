Understanding Tokens

What are tokens? Tokens are the basic units that AI models use to process text. They can be words, parts of words, or even punctuation marks.

  • Input Tokens: The text you send (your prompt + conversation history)
  • Output Tokens: The AI's response text
  • Pricing: You pay for both input and output tokens (~$0.50 per 1M tokens)
  • Example: "Hello world!" ‚âà 3 tokens

Max Response Tokens: Controls how long the AI's reply can be. Higher = longer responses but more cost.

Making the Most of Personalities

  • Match Your Need: Use Detective for analysis, Zen Master for philosophy, Scientist for technical topics

  • Play Along: Engage with their unique speaking style and perspective
  • Experiment: Ask the same question to different personalities for varied insights
  • Context Matters: Each personality remembers your conversation until you switch
